Our Story

About Mohsin Studio

What started as one Vediographer with a single camera has grown into Pasrur's trusted name for wedding and event coverage — built one happy family at a time.

Our Journey

From one camera to a full studio

2005

The beginning

Mohsin Studio was founded with a single camera and a passion for capturing life's most precious moments. Our journey began by covering local family events and weddings in Pasrur.

2010

Building the team

As our reputation grew, experienced photographers and videographers joined the studio, allowing us to cover larger weddings and multiple events with professional quality.

2018

Expanding services

We introduced cinematic videography, drone coverage, advanced photo editing, albums, and complete wedding media solutions to provide a premium experience.

2023

Growing Stronger

Mohsin Studio expanded its team and successfully covered hundreds of weddings, engagements, birthdays, and corporate events across Sialkot and surrounding cities.

2026

Today

Today, Mohsin Studio is a trusted name in photography and videography, proudly serving families across Punjab with over 20 years of experience, 500+ happy clients, and a commitment to capturing memories that last forever
